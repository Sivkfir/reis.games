Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

REI Games Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website reis.games. This page explains what information we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, or payment details directly.

We use third-party analytics tools (only with your consent) that automatically collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Buttons and links clicked
  • Device type, browser, and approximate location (country/city)
  • Session recordings and heatmaps (mouse movements, scrolling)

No personally identifiable information is included in any data collected.

2. ANALYTICS SERVICES

We use the following third-party analytics services, loaded only after you give consent:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — collects anonymized usage statistics. Analytics data is retained for up to 26 months per Google's default settings. Google Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Clarity — records anonymized session replays and heatmaps. Microsoft Privacy Policy

Both services use cookies to function. No personally identifiable information is shared with us by either service.

3. PLANNED FUTURE SERVICES

We may in the future use Google AdSense to display contextually relevant advertisements. If and when we do, we will update this Privacy Policy and request your explicit consent before activating any advertising cookies. Currently, no advertising cookies are set on this site.

4. COOKIES

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential cookies — we store your cookie preference choices in your browser's localStorage. These are always active and required for the consent system to work.
  • Analytics cookies — set by Google Analytics 4 and Microsoft Clarity to track anonymous usage patterns. These are only loaded after you consent.
  • Advertising cookies — not currently in use.

For a full breakdown of every cookie we use, see our Cookie Policy.

You can manage your preferences at any time via the Cookie Settings option in the site footer. You can also clear cookies at any time through your browser settings. Declining analytics cookies does not affect your ability to use the site or play our games.

5. HOW WE USE THE DATA

We use collected data solely to:

  • Understand how visitors use our site and games
  • Improve gameplay and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for advertising or commercial purposes.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

REI Games Studio is a game studio founded by a young creator. Our games are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Analytics data is collected anonymously and contains no personal identifiers.

7. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent for analytics at any time (use Cookie Settings in the footer)
  • Request portability of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Since we do not collect personal data directly, most requests can be fulfilled by clearing your browser cookies and localStorage. For any other requests, contact us at the address below.

8. DATA RETENTION

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Analytics data held by Google Analytics is retained for up to 26 months by default. Microsoft Clarity retains session data per its own retention policy. You can request deletion directly from those services.

9. CONTACT

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: hello@reis.games
