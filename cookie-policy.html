Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how REI Games Studio uses cookies and similar technologies on reis.games. By using our site you agree to the use of essential cookies. For all other cookie types, we ask for your consent via the cookie banner.

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and settings. Some cookies are stored only for the duration of your browsing session; others remain on your device for a set period.

We also use localStorage — a browser storage mechanism similar to cookies — to save your cookie preference choices.

2. HOW WE USE COOKIES

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your cookie preferences so we don't ask every visit
  • Understand how visitors use our site and games (with your consent)
  • Improve gameplay, navigation, and the overall user experience
  • Identify and fix technical issues

We do not use cookies to serve personalised advertisements at this time. Advertising cookies are listed below as "not currently in use" — we will request your explicit consent before activating them in the future.

4. MANAGING YOUR COOKIE PREFERENCES

You can change your cookie preferences at any time using the Cookie Settings button:

Cookie Settings

You can also clear all cookies and localStorage data through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data

Note that clearing cookies will reset your preferences and the cookie banner will reappear on your next visit.
