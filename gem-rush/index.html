🎮 How to Play

Goal: Collect as many gems as possible in 60 seconds.

Each gem disappears after ~4 seconds — catch them before they vanish!

Missing a gem breaks your combo. Keep collecting to multiply your score.


Mobile: Use the joystick (bottom-left) to move.

Desktop: WASD or arrow keys to move.
